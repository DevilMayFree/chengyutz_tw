]]> The SEMICON Taiwan 2023 International Semiconductor Exhibition is set to kick off on September 6 at the Nangang Exhibition Hall. PlayNitride, a pioneer in advancing MicroLED commercialization, is geared up to showcase its comprehensive solutions for MicroLED displays. The company promises to deliver an artful immersion experience for attendees, coupled with carefully curated booth designs. The exhibit will feature three main product solution zones: modular large-size MicroLED displays ready for mass production; miniaturized MicroLED micro-displays showcased in module formats for AR applications; wearables and transparent consumer electronics applications, as well as a demo in specialized darkroom.

The booth space is categorized into distinct thematic areas, colored in red, green, and blue — each representing different display themes.

Red Zone: Fashion Meets Function

In the red zone, visitors can experience the integration of optical technology with AR glasses, featuring 0.49-inch micro-sized MicroLED displays presented in a modular fashion. With 4536PPI and brightness levels exceeding 100,000 nits, these displays exemplify the lightweight, high-resolution, and luminosity that MicroLED technology can offer.

Green Zone: Eco-Friendly Tech

MicroLEDs are known for their environmentally friendly attributes, such as simplified structure and reduced energy consumption. These attributes are showcased in the green tropical rainforest zone. Products ranging from 1.58-inch wearable displays featuring different MicroLED technologies (RGB or Quantum Dot color conversion) to 9.38-inch transparent touch displays are on show, allowing for a deeper understanding of MicroLED applications and mass production capabilities.

Blue Zone: Excellence in Low-Light Environments

The blue darkroom area will demonstrate the outstanding performance of MicroLEDs not just in bright background but also in low-light conditions. Visitors will experience firsthand the remarkable contrast and performance in dark states that these displays offer.

PlayNitride aims to transcend the boundaries of traditional technology exhibitions at SEMICON Taiwan, offering an artful experience that goes beyond simply showcasing MicroLED features. Attendees can immerse themselves in a multifaceted display of usability, artistry, and technology. The event promises to serve as a bridge from the specifications of traditional displays to the advanced applications of future technologies, thus highlighting the remarkable capabilities and features of this “ultimate display technology.”

]]> On May 25, 2023, the Society for Information Display (SID) announced the results of the People’s Choice Awards during Display Week. This year, the voting process was categorized based on the exhibiting companies’ technologies, including MicroLED, OLED, equipment, LCD, automotive, and other specific fields, intensifying the competition in the MicroLED category.

PlayNitride stood out among the participants and won three prestigious awards, establishing itself as one of the highlights of the event. Firstly, PlayNitride received the “Best Medium Booth Award” for its outstanding booth design. The booth featured a color scheme of red, blue, and green, creating an immersive artistic atmosphere that visually captivated and delighted the audience.

Secondly, PlayNitride showcased a remarkable 166-inch seamless MicroLED modular display, winning the “Best MicroLED-Based Technology Award.” This large-scale display boasted an impressive brightness of over 2000 nits, captivating the audience with its dazzling effect. Its exceptional visual performance and technological innovation sparked great interest and inquiries from the industry and attendees.

Additionally, PlayNitride presented a 0.49-inch MicroLED micro-display with an astonishing pixel density of 4536PPI and high brightness, earning the “Best AR/VR/MR Demo/Product Award.” This display, accompanied by two different optical modules, allowed visitors to experience the effects of augmented reality glasses firsthand.

PlayNitride demonstrated its technical prowess and innovative capabilities at the SID exhibition, solidifying its leading position in the MicroLED display field. Their products not only achieved significant breakthroughs in technical performance but also showcased a strong commitment to innovative design and visitor experience.

The Chairman of PlayNitride expressed great honor in receiving recognition at the SID exhibition and was delighted to see MicroLED displays becoming the focal point of the event. The Chairman also acknowledged the emergence of numerous companies introducing related products and solutions, indicating the industry’s shaping future. Furthermore, he encouraged the PlayNitride team to think critically and overcome market demands and challenges, enabling accelerated market penetration for MicroLED products.

]]> Today, November 10th, 2022, the Audit Committee and the Board of Directors of PlayNitride Inc. had approved the proposed cooperation agreement between PlayNitride Display Co., Ltd. (hereinafter “PlayNitride Display”), a subsidiary of PlayNitride Inc. , and Epistar Corporation (hereinafter “Epistar”). PlayNitride Display and Epistar have been negotiating to enter a cooperation agreement (hereinafter “Cooperation Agreement”), under which PlayNitride Display shall collaborate with Epistar in building a 6” Micro LED Chip-On-Wafer (COW) production line at the site designated by Epistar. In consideration for PlayNitride Display’s collaboration and services provided under the Cooperation Agreement, Epistar shall pay PlayNitride Display a total of NT$600 million.

The Board of Directors further authorize the Chairman of PlayNitride Inc., or a person designated by the Chairman, to negotiate, amend and execute on behalf of PlayNitride Display the Cooperation Agreement and relevant documents with Epistar, and to handle any matters or acts relevant to carry out the purposes of, but not otherwise provided or addressed under, the Cooperation Agreement with full power and authority.

PlayNitride Display is the 100%-owned subsidiary of PlayNitride Inc. The company continues to launch various Micro LED products, including large-size panels, automotive products, wearable devices, etc. Epistar is a leading global LED chip manufacturer which has formed a long-term partnership with PlayNitride Display. To meet the need of the fast-growing market and the strong demand of production capacity, PlayNitride Display had carefully evaluated and communicated with the world’s best LED technology companies for Micro LED related cooperation. PlayNitride Display expects that under the cooperation, the capacity and efficiency of both companies can be improved, accelerating the development of Micro LED industry. PlayNitride Inc. will continue to invest in the development of Micro LED, the ultimate display technology.

PlayNitride will exhibit at SID 2022 in San Jose McEnery Convention Center from May 10th to 12th. Along with the trend of MicroLED commercialization, PlayNitride is going to exhibit three different scenarios to bring the visitors to experience the MicroLED world.

First, the 132-inch PixeLED Matrix seamless tiling MicroLED modular display as a welcome banner, showing unlimited contrast ratio, 116% NTSC, 2000nits brightness to catch the eyes in a short viewing distance than ever. The delicate and vivid picture quality can break the limitation of large signage boards, allowing all practical technology combined with art to become a smart street scene.

In the middle of the booth is the automotive area, and the center console module demonstrates different usage of the MicroLED display. 9.38-inch transparent MicroLED display in the first debut shows 65%~70% transparency which is required by the automotive specification. Brand new 11.6-inch MicroLED display in 24:9 ratio and 2.5K resolutions can satisfy the requirement of the automotive market. Besides, 1.58-inch passive matrix square MicroLED display can be used for wearable applications and for showing brief information on cars. The MicroLED display automotive demonstration could bring more ideas to visitors.

Surrounding the booth are concept demonstrations of smart home applications and consumer electronics products. The 7.56-inch QD MicroLED display as a mural sided by another FHD resolution LCD display shows an unlimited contrast ratio and 120% NTSC performance and the difference of power consumption to prove how eco-friendly the MicroLED display is. 1.39-inch in circular and 1.58-inch in square show high brightness and resolution as smart watches which are free from sunlight interference. Furthermore, flexible MicroLED displays are packed into fashionable designs.

MicroLED plays an important role in Metaverse. There is an independent area for the visitors to experience the AR application by the 0.49-inch, 4536ppi, FHD resolution (1920*1080), full-color, awarded µ-PixeLED MicroLED micro-display which is the highest specification in the world.

PlayNitride provides different MicroLED solutions for different application scenarios at SID Display Week 2022, which can bring the visitors to experience the advantages of what MicroLED can be. No matter the upgrade performance of traditional display or further display applications, MicroLED shows great performance as the “ultimate display” everyone expected.
